Quality Control Lot Traceability Search

Purpose
Provides the ability to track Delphi and Vendor Part/Serial Number combinations.
 

About this page
  • The From and To Date fields are required
  • Time Stamp represents the Date that the label was produced.
  • The error message, "Master Pack record does not exist" indicates that the record is flagged as a Master Pack record but the Master Pack record does not exist.

Searching
  1. Click on the calendar image to the right of the From Date or enter a valid date in the form of mm/dd/yyyy
  2. Click on the calendar image to the right of the To Date or enter a valid date in the form of mm/dd/yyyy
    • To view data for ALL Delphi Part Numbers/Assembly Lines, leave the default values for part number and assembly line and click on the Search Button.
    • To view the data for a Specific Delphi Part Number, enter the Part Number and click on Search.
    • To view the data for a Specific Part Number/Assembly Line combination, enter both Part Number and Assembly Line.
    • To view all assembly lines for a specific Delphi Part Number, just enter the Part Number and leave Assembly Line as “ASM”.
  3. After the Search button is clicked on, all records pertaining to the search criteria specified, will be displayed. Records that have a plus sign next to them indicates that the record is a Master Pack. To view all Standard Pack records for the Master Pack, click on the plus sign. A minus sign indicates the Master Pack record has been expanded out (only appears on the last record for which the plus sign was clicked). If no plus or minus sign exists next to a record, that indicates the record is a Standard Pack record.
